# Mizano - The Ultimate Navigation Manual

Welcome to the **Mizano**, the cognitive nerve center of the Mizano platform. This interface is designed with **Tactile Intelligence**, leveraging human cognitive behavior to make information discovery not just fast, but instinctive.

## Design Philosophy: "Horizon Flux"
The Mizano operates on a "Layered Consciousness" model. By stacking critical controls at the bottom (where your thumbs naturally rest) and using collapsible "folding" levels, we maximize screen real estate for content while keeping powerful filters instantly accessible. "Horizon Flux" describes the smooth, predictable motion of UI levels as they reveal themselves in response to your intent.

---

## 1. The Bottom Deck (Level A)
The foundation of the app, featuring 7 key pillars of navigation:

- **Activity Axis (Man Icon)**: Your gateway to the world of sports and hobbies.
- **Spatial Locator (Location Icon)**: Pins your experience to your current city or neighborhood.
- **The Panelist (3 Lines)**: A clearing-house for all 17 specialized panels.
- **The Seer (Search Icon)**: Instant indexing of anything on the platform.
- **The Creator (+ Icon)**: Proactive building of goals, events, and teams.
- **The Pulse (Notifications)**: A white interactive notifier sitting on a deep #363636 rectangle.
- **The Hub (Hamburger Button)**: Access to your profile, settings, and this manual.

---

## 2. Advanced Filtering Hierarchy

### Level B: Activity Stream
Our "Smart Syntax" filter label reads your intent. 
- If you select **Soccer** + **Recruiting**, the app shows exactly that.
- If you select more, the UI tidies up into a **"# filters"** counter to keep your workspace clean.

### Level C: Temporal Controls
- **Time Carousel**: Scroll through **Past, Yesterday, Today, Upcoming, All** to jump through the timeline. Responsive and immediate.
- **Date Picker**: A folding white bar reveals **Day Tiles**, making specific date targeting as easy as a swipe. "Today" is always visually anchored.

---

## 3. Spatial Intelligence (The Sub-Header)
Located just below the top bar, the **Location Chip** acts as your spatial anchor.
- **Vanish Logic**: Tapping the Chip (e.g., GC • All) opens a dynamic menu that narrows your world. Once a city is selected, all broader options "vanish," revealing only relevant neighborhoods or villages.
- **Neural Counter**: The "+888" counter on the right provides immediate feedback on data density, letting you know how many opportunities exist within your current filter scope.

---

## 4. How to Make the Most of Mizano
- **Location Pinning**: Tap the ISO chip (e.g., GC) to instantly swap neighborhoods without losing your spot.
- **Collapsible categories**: In the "Add" and "Activity" menus, look for the +/- buttons. We categorize 100+ sports to ensure you aren't overwhelmed.
- **Horizon Hiding**: Experience total immersion. The navigation deck and top bar hide when you scroll down to focus on content, reappearing only when you "look back" (scroll up).
